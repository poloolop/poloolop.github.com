"Greed captures the essence of the evolutionary spirit." - Gordon Gekko
Winning is also sometimes confused with 'Money'
"It's not a question of enough, pal. It's a Zero Sum game – somebody wins, somebody loses. Money itself isn't lost or made, it's simply transferred – from one perception to another." - Gordon Gekko



Thankfully, winning is neither of the above. 
India Today Conclave 2014 is asking this question - 
What winning means to you?
For me it's simple - 
Winning means Everything ... and more. 


Himangini Singh Yadu with Sushmita Sen -
after winning Miss Asia Pacific World 2012

It means everything however it not about winning a 'Lucky Draw' contest.
It does means winning in a competitive contest.
It does mean winning 22 medals at Olympics (Michael Phelps)




















In Wikipedia, Winning (of Victory) is described as  something which is accompanied with strong feelings of elation. And although, our society has a 'Winner takes all' approach, what matters more than anything else is to give your best, and --
Playing to win
So keep that in mind, when you dive into your next battle. I hope you emerge victorious.

Go. Win.
