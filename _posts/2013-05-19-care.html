Health care in India is pretty archaic. We have some of the best skilled medical professionals, but that hasn't truly translated into anything special in terms of healthcare standards or sophistication.

So how does modern healthcare affects our lives?
It has surely simplified a ton of stuff for us simpletons. There are some outlets offering to save electronic medical records, and want people to enrol for bi-annual health checkups. So as to prevent as many last minute surprises as possible. There's always the question of affordability and what all is covered under insurance. I am always sceptical of entering a hospital.



With increasing number of health monitoring and tracking gadgets, hopefully we will be better prepared for medical exigencies.
