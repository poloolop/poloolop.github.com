Lately I've been obsessed with yogurt. A day is incomplete without at least one portion of yogurt in your diet. It also gives you ~30% of your daily requirements of calcium.


Yogurt is of immense importance in the diet of a vegetarian. As a thumb rule, never skip your daily portion of yogurt.
