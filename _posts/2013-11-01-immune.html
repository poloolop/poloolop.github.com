Daubr Chyawanprash created a thread on Indiblogger to talk about our Immune system and it's role to prevent diseases. 


Their big question is "How you think we can build a stronger, healthier and a more immune India?'

Play

Well to start with, for being healthy and strong, kids need to play and be active in some sport. For me it was Badminton, 'Gully' Cricket and Football. Here's a quote 
“Sports do not build character. They reveal it.” - John Wooden

Green

Eat lots of Green vegetables. I am a vegetarian all my life, and am now trying to be a 'Vegan', however even for Non-vegetarians, eating lots of fresh vegetables is recommended.

Meditate 

I try to meditate at least once a week. It helps one to clear one's mind and helps our system find a bearing in this vast universe. 

There are plenty of other points which also help in improving health and our immune system. I have mainly listed my top three from them. Indian kids need to be stronger to take on bigger  challenges facing mankind in the future.
