Rise and Shine

Sunsilk is asking me to blog again, this time on reason to recharge one's hair. Last time I wrote about their TRESemmé professional line of products, which I continue to use to date. They were kind enough to send me a sample, I was hooked ever since.

For me the key reason to keep one's hair recharged, is to feel good. I like my hair light and silky, and TRESemmé helps me keep them that way.




Shine

Pink Floyd's song "Shine on you crazy diamond" comes to my mind when I am thinking about importance of energy, bounce, shine and health of hair. Over the years, importance of taking care of hair has been reinforced to me by my mother, sister and wife. 



Buy

I'd recommend TRESemmé hair products to anyone. The pricing is also reasonable, not the obscene amounts professional products usually charge.
