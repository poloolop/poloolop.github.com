She left her job in Management Consulting to go teach Seventh Grade Maths class. She then went on to become a psychologist.

Her conclusion after years of observation and methodical studies conducted? She asked -  'Who is successful here and why?'. Her answer. Four letters
GRIT = "Passion and perseverance for very long term goals. Grit is having stamina. Grit is sticking with your future, day in day out, not just for a month but for years. Grit is living life like it is a marathon, not a sprint." - Angela Lee Duckworth



Online dictionaries define Grit as 'Indomitable spirit' or 'resolve to do something difficult or unpleasant'.

Her closing words - "We need to be gritty about getting our kids grittier"
