I got shampoo and conditioner from TRESemmé about a week back and have been using the same regularly for the same period. Following are my observations -


  1. Shampoo lathers well with limited quantity applied
  2. Shampoo is more or less sufficient for daily use. I am not a regular user of conditioner products and only shampoo worked well for me.
  3. I didn't get any dandruff issues, scratchiness, dryness etc. in using this shampoo. Sometimes shampoos can be harsh but TRESemmé felt in the milder spectrum of hair products I have used
  4. Conditioner - I did try out their conditioner and was pleasantly surprised. My earlier experience with conditioners was no-difference felt i.e. it didn't improve my hair situation, but that was not the case with this one. Again, a small quantity felt sufficient to cover my hair and it improved the way my hair felt through the day. Hair were softer and looked much better.

I'd recommend TRESemmé hair products to anyone. The pricing is also reasonable, not the obscene amounts professional products usually charge. Do give it a try and let me know your experience with TRESemmé. Some pics -
