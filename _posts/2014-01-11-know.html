He agreed. We all have our, 'If I had studied ... instead' moments.

British Council is asking the same question on their site 'Knowledge is Great' -
"If you could study / specialize in any subject of your choice in the UK, what would it be, where and why"?
For me the answer is simple. Having read about Anthropology and Human Evolution by Naturalist Charles Darwin, it has to be Anthropology at Cambridge, which is also Darwin's Alma Mater.


"It is not the strongest of the species that survives, nor the most intelligent that survives. It is the one that is the most adaptable to change." - Charles Darwin
 My other inspiration to study Anthropology comes from none other than Pulitzer Prize winning author Jared Diamond. I reviewed his book Collapse here. 
"The crux of success or failure as a society is to know which core values to hold on to and which ones to discard and replace with new values when times change." - Jared Diamond in Book Collapse
The reason for choosing Cambridge is also straight forward and needs no explanation. Their reputation in world of education is unmatched, and their standards of excellence are known by one and all.

If I were to turn back time and choose my subject again, Arts and Humanities would be my first choice, and Anthropology as specialization. But alas, there's no way of doing that now. However one can always keep learning new things anytime, anywhere. Go figure and learn.
